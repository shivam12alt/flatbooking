Research and Selection

Online flat booking refers to the process of reserving or booking a flat or apartment through an online platform or website without physically visiting the property or interacting with a real estate agent in person. It involves using digital tools and services to browse available flats, view property details, check availability, and make a reservation or booking online.

Availability and Booking

After making the booking, you may receive a confirmation email or notification with the details of your reservation. Some platforms may require additional verification steps, such as identity verification or providing relevant documents.

Check-in and Check-out

On the scheduled date of your arrival, follow the instructions provided by the property owner or manager for check-in procedures. This may include meeting a representative, accessing a lockbox, or using a keyless entry system. Similarly, during check-out, follow the instructions for returning keys or any other requirements specified.
